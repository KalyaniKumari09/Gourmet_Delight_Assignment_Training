Gourmet Delight offers a fine dining experience with exquisite dishes crafted by our renowned chefs. Our restaurant provides a cozy atmosphere where you can enjoy delicious food with your loved ones.

From our prized pavlova to our magnificent mud cake, our range of creations will have your dessert menu covered.

To keep our treats fresh and minimise waste, they are pre-portioned and frozen.
